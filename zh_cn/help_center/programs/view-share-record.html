After you have earned a certificate, you can view your certificates on the My Records page. The My Records page displays your course completion status for any courses that are part of a program, such as a MicroMasters. On the My Records page, you can choose to share your course record with an institution that accepts that course as a step towards admission or completion of a degree program.

Your My Records page exists only if you have earned a certificate in a course that is part of a program.

To share your course record, follow these steps.

  1. At the top of any page, select Dashboard from the dropdown menu icon next to your username, then select Profile. If you are already on your Dashboard page, select the Profile tab.
  2. On the Profile tab, select View My Records.
  3. On the My Records page, find the program record that you want to share and select View Program Record.
  4. On the Record page for the course, select Send Learner Record.

After you select Send Learner Record, the institution you shared your program record with can view the record for that program. For detailed information about who can see your program record and what they can see, see When I share my course record, what information is shared?
