You can view and share your program record on the My Records page of the LMS.Â When you share your program record, the institution you share it with receives the following information about your performance in each of the program's courses.

  • Your name.
  • The course name.
  • The name of the program that the course is a part of.
  • The institution that offered the course.
  • The number of times you started the course as a verified learner.
  • The date a certificate was awarded to you for the course.
  • Your percent grade for the course. If you started the course more than once, this will be the highest earned grade that you earned.
  • Your letter grade for the course. If you started the course more than once, this will be the highest earned grade that you earned.

After you have shared your program record, you cannot retract the information that you shared.
